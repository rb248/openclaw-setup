## The Quiet Hiring Reality

**What's Changed:**
- Recruiters no longer post the best jobs publicly
- LinkedIn's AI searches 1.3B profiles for matches
- Your activity and content = your discoverability
- 2-3x per week posting minimum; daily is ideal

**The Algorithm Looks For:**
1. **Activity** - Are you active on the platform?
2. **Industry** - What industry do you talk about?
3. **Skills** - What problems do you solve?
4. **Consistency** - Do you post regularly about the same topics?

## Content Strategy

### The 2-3 Pain Pillars Approach

**RB's Focus Areas:**
1. **MLOps & Production ML** - Building systems that actually work
2. **Autonomous Systems** - LiDAR, 3D vision, robotics
3. **Applied NLP** - Chatbots, LLMs in production

**Posting Schedule:**
- **Minimum:** 2-3x per week
- **Ideal:** Monday-Friday daily (the "streak")
- **Best times:** 8-9 AM or 12-1 PM CET (Tue-Thu optimal)
- **Word count:** 300-500 words
- **Hashtags:** Exactly 3, skill/industry specific

### Content Types

1. **Interview Answers** (40%)
   - Answer questions you'd face in interviews
   - 20 questions = 4 weeks of content
   - Example: "Tell me about a time you improved model performance"

2. **Skills → Masterpiece** (30%)
   - Don't list skills; show what you CREATE
   - Example: "I don't just know PyTorch, I build..."

3. **Industry Commentary** (20%)
   - Hot takes on ML/AI trends
   - EU AI Act, autonomous driving developments
   - LLM production challenges

4. **Journey/Behind-the-Scenes** (10%)
   - Bangalore to Aachen story
   - Learning moments, mentorship insights

## Key Reminders for RB
