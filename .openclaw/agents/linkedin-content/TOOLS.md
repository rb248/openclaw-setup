## Content Creation Tools

### Hashtag Strategy
- Use exactly **3 hashtags** per post (no more, no less)
- 2 specific to pain pillar, 1 broader
- Examples for RB:
  - Technical: #MachineLearning #MLOps #Python
  - Domain: #AutonomousDriving #LiDAR #Robotics
  - Career: #DataScientist #RWTH #Germany
  - Community: #LearningInPublic #TechCommunity

### Post Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday-Thursday
- **Best times:** 8-9 AM or 12-1 PM CET
- **Streak strategy:** Monday-Friday posting
- Take weekends off

### Content Specifications
- **Length:** 300-500 words (for depth and algorithm recognition)
- **Format:** Text + photo of yourself OR video
- **Hashtags:** Exactly 3
- **CTA:** End with question for engagement

## Target Company Research

### Finding Target Companies
1. Use ChatGPT: "Find 20 companies in Germany hiring ML engineers for [autonomous systems/MLOps/NLP]"
2. Search LinkedIn Jobs for "Machine Learning Engineer Germany"
3. Look at RWTH career portal alumni companies
4. Check German AI startups (AI Bundesverband member list)
5. Research autonomous driving companies in Germany

### Finding People to Follow
For each target company:
1. Go to company LinkedIn page
2. Click "People"
3. Search: "Machine Learning" or "Data Science"
4. Find 3-5 people in relevant departments
5. Follow them (don't connect yet)
6. Turn on notifications for their posts

### Commenting Best Practices
- **Do:** Add to conversation, ask questions, share relevant experience
- **Don't:** Say "congratulations" or "I agree" or "great post"
- **Formula:** "This makes sense + [your addition/perspective/question]"
- **Goal:** Start a dialogue, be memorable

## Quick Reference: The Daily Routine (15 mins)

## Emergency Content Ideas

When you don't know what to post:

1. **Answer an interview question** (see list above)
2. **Share a recent learning** (course, article, project)
3. **Explain a technical concept simply** (your target audience will appreciate it)
4. **Share a project retrospective** (what worked, what didn't)
5. **Comment on industry news** (EU AI Act, new LLM release, etc.)
6. **Tell your career story** (Bangalore to Aachen)
7. **Share a mentorship moment** (Techlabs experience)
8. **Explain a technical decision** (why you chose X over Y)
