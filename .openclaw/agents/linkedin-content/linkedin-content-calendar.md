# LinkedIn Content Calendar - RB (Updated Strategy)

## The Quiet Hiring Strategy

**Core Principle:** Recruiters aren't posting the best jobs anymore. They're using LinkedIn's AI to proactively search for candidates. Your content = your 24/7 interview.

**Posting Cadence:**
- **Minimum:** 2-3x per week
- **Ideal:** Monday-Friday daily (the "streak")
- **Best times:** 8-9 AM or 12-1 PM CET (Tue-Thu optimal)
- **Format:** 300-500 words + photo/video + 3 hashtags

**The 2-3 Pain Pillars for RB:**
1. **MLOps & Production ML** - Building systems that actually work at scale
2. **Autonomous Systems** - LiDAR, 3D vision, perception systems
3. **Applied NLP** - Chatbots, LLMs, conversational AI in production
